Before we start building anything, let's talk about what Barba actually is and why we're using it. We'll look at the 'problem' it solves, how it changes the way your website handles navigation, and what that means for your animations. By the end of this lesson you'll have a clear picture of what Barba does and what your role is in making the magic happen.
