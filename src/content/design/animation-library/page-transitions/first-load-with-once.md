The "once" animation runs on the very first page load, before any transitions happen. It's your chance to make a first impression. In this lesson we'll look at how to set up a dedicated once animation, separate from your regular enter transition.
### Lesson notes
#### What is the once animation?
When someone first lands on your site, there's no previous page. No leave animation. Just the page appearing. The `once` function in Barba lets you handle this moment differently.
#### When to use it
Use it when you want a special first-visit experience:
- A loader or intro sequence
- A logo animation that plays once
- A more dramatic reveal than your standard enter
- Etc...
